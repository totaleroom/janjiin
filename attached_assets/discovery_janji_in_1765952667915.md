# Janji.in - Product Discovery & Validation

**Mission:** Mengubah cara UKM jasa Indonesia mengatur jadwal, dari "ribet WhatsApp" jadi "otomatis cuan".

## 1. Problem Statement & Pain Score
**Problem:** UKM Jasa di Indonesia kehilangan 20-30% potensi revenue karena manajemen booking yang manual via WhatsApp (slow response, double booking, no-show tanpa DP, dan admin burnout). customer malas menunggu balasan admin hanya untuk tahu slot kosong.

**Pain Score: 9/10**
- **High Frequency:** Terjadi setiap hari, setiap ada calon customer chat.
- **High Friction:** Chat bolak-balik "Halo kak, jam 3 kosong?" "Yah penuh kak" "Jam 4?" "Bentar dicek"... (Customer kabur).
- **Financial Loss:** Customer "ghosting" (no-show) padahal slot sudah dikunci = rugi waktu & uang.

## 2. Target Persona
### A. The "Overwhelmed" Owner (Primary)
- **Role:** Pemilik Salon / Barbershop / Klinik Gigi Kecil.
- **Demographic:** Usia 25-40, Tech-savvy tapi sibuk operasional.
- **Pain:** Hape bunyi terus (WA), sering salah catat jadwal di buku/Excel, karyawan sering bingung jadwal.
- **Goal:** Mau sistem yang "jalan sendiri", terima booking 24/7, dan terima DP otomatis biar gak ditipu customer.

### B. The "Sat-Set" Customer
- **Role:** Gen Z / Millennial (Consumer).
- **Behavior:** Males nanya harga/slot via chat. Kalau admin lama balas, langsung pindah ke kompetitor.
- **Goal:** Lihat slot, klik booking, bayar/DP, datang. Selesai. Gak mau basa-basi.

### C. The "Admin" Staff
- **Role:** Kasir / Resepsionis.
- **Pain:** Dimarahin bos kalau double booking. Pusing rekap chat WA satu-satu ke buku tamu.
- **Goal:** Cuma mau lihat kalender hari ini, siapa yang datang, siapa yang belum bayar.

## 3. Value Proposition Canvas
**"Website booking instan untuk jasamu, terintegrasi WhatsApp & QRIS, anti no-show dengan fitur DP otomatis."**
*(The "Calendly + Tokopedia" for Indonesian Services)*

## 5. Our Differentiation
**Positioning:** "Bukan POS yang ribet, bukan sekedar antrian. Kami adalah *Personal Assistant* digital untuk bisnismu."

**Key Differentiators:**
1.  **WhatsApp First:** Notifikasi booking, reminder H-1, dan follow-up review otomatis via WA (bukan Email).
2.  **DP & QRIS Integration:** Validasi booking dengan DP via QRIS (DANA/Gopay/OVO) instan. Anti ghosting.
3.  **Super Simple Setup:** "Jadi dalam 5 menit". Tidak perlu setup inventory, payroll, dll. Cuma Jasa, Staff, & Jadwal.
4.  **Local Context:** Support istilah "DP", "Lunasin di tempat", "Reschedule max 1x".

## 6. Success Metrics (MVP - First 6 Months)
1.  **Total Bookings Processed:** 1,000+ bookings/month real-time.
2.  **Active Merchants:** 50+ UKM aktif (min. 5 booking/minggu).
3.  **No-Show Reduction Rate:** Merchant melaporkan penurunan no-show hingga 80% (validated via survey).

## 7. MVP Scope (IN vs OUT)
**IN (Version 1.0):**
1.  **Merchant Page:** Profil bisnis, Menu Jasa, Pilihan Staff/Jam.
2.  **Booking Flow:** Pilih Jasa -> Pilih Staff/Jam -> Input Info (Nama/WA) -> Bayar DP (QRIS) / Pay Later.
3.  **Admin Dashboard (Mobile Web):** Lihat Calendar, Approve/Reject (jika manual), Input Manual Booking (untuk yang walk-in/WA manual).
4.  **WhatsApp Notifications:** OTOMATIS Kirim konfirmasi booking ke Admin & Customer.
5.  **Basic Reporting:** Total booking & est. revenue bulan ini.

**OUT (Phase 2+):**
- Inventory Management (Shampo, Oli, dll) -> *Too complex.*
- Payroll / Komisi Staff -> *Later.*
- Mobile App Native (Android/iOS) -> *Web-based first (PWA).*
- Marketing Blast -> *Focus on utility first.*

## 8. Pricing Realistis Indonesia
**Model:** Freemium + Transaction/Subscription Hybrid.

1.  **TIER 1: GRATIS (Starter)**
    - Max 2 booking/bulan.
    - 1 Staff / Calendar.
    - Manual Reminder (Template WA).
    - *Cocok untuk: Les privat, tukang cukur solo.*
    - **Harga: Rp 0,-**

2.  **TIER 2: MAJU (Pro)**
    - Unlimited Booking.
    - Up to 5 Staff.
    - **WhatsApp Reminder Otomatis** (Killer Feature!).
    - Terima DP via QRIS (Platform fee 1-2%).
    - *Cocok untuk: Barbershop ramai, Salon kecil, Dokter gigi.*
    - **Harga: Rp 49.000 / bulan** (Harga "2 mangkok bakso").

3.  **TIER 3: SUKSES (Business)**
    - Unlimited Staff.
    - Multi-cabang (soon).
    - Custom Branding (White label/Hilangkan logo Janji.in).
    - Data ekspor pelannggan.
    - **Harga: Rp 149.000 / bulan**.
